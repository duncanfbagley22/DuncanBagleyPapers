Week 12 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 11 Ago 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This was a good week, mixed with a surprise. We found some new investigators and we got out and worked hard. I was starting to see some progress with Elder Prado, so I am kind of sad to have a change, but excited at the same time. Me and Elder Christensen were companions in the mtc, so it´ll be fun to see how much we´ve grown and changed since then. I know that we are going to work hard and be obedient. One thing that I personally think is that we should stay in Cobija, me and Elder Christensen, and the other 2 should go to Ingavi. I have explained it to Elder Weekes why it makes more sense, at least in my opinion. However, if we do stay in Ingavi I wont be dissappointed either. I am started to try to get out in the field more, and it is a lot easier now because we actually have investigators and people to go visit. We were able to give a blessing to a less active member yesterday and that was a cool experience. I enjoy working in the office, but the field work is a lot more gratifying. I like helping people to change their lives through the gospel. We also had a lesson with the nephew of Elder Vladimir Campero, an old area 70. He is a catholic deacon, but he is really interested in the book of mormon, so we have focused on that. I am excited to keep progressing in the work, whether it be Cobija or Ingavi.
    
Thanks for everything
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

Thanks again for all the hard work and long hours you put in as financial secretary. I thought you would jump for joy at being reassigned to Elder Christiansen. It's interesting how the reactions sometimes surprise me.
--Pte. Hansen
