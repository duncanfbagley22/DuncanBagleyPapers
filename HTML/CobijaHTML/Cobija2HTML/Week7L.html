Week 7 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 07 Jul 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    I really loved the conference this week. The spirit was great and testified to me that you and Hna Hansen really are the people to lead this mission. You both are going to be great. This week was really long for me and very busy. We tried to get out in the field on the weekend, but we have a really rich area, so we are going to have to take new approaches to working. I am trying to help Elder Prado. He isnt my hardest companion, and he is really easy to get along with, but at times he is difficult to work with. My old companion was 28, and Elder Prado is 19 and acts very young, so it requires some adjusting. He is a great person though, and wants to be a great missionary, he just lacks some drive sometimes. We both really want to get the work going in our area, so we are going to try to work more in the field. I am trying to find more ways to make my job more spiritual, as times I can get caught up in the worldly side of my job and I lose a little bit of spirituality. Anyways, thank you for all that you do and I look forward to our interview in the coming weeks.
    
love,
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

Elder Bagley, you are a joy to work with. You accomplish an amazing amount of work and keep such a great attitude. Thanks for all you do.
--Pte. Hansen
