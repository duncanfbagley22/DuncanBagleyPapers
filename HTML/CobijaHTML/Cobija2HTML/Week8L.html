Week 8 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 14 Jul 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This was a long and somewhat stressful week, but it turned out ok in the end. It was tough to get anything going on Sunday because of the world cup. I am so glad it is over now, but the people wont be as busy. We had a couple great miracles happen this week that I would like to share. First was on Saturday I found a receipt I was looking for. It doesnt sound that cool, but it was basically finding a needle in a haystack and it saves us around 1000bs, so I was really happy about it. The other one happened to me and Elder Christensen. After we dropped off Elder Suarez at the airport in the morning, we got home and found out we had to go back because he had a fee to pay and it looked like he would miss his flight. Well we ran back to the airport as fast as we could, but everything was already taken care of. We met this member at security that said that he and his family saw Elder Suarez in a panic and went to help him. They even ended up paying the fee, around 100bs, and wouldnt let us pay them back. They were the perfect people to be there and were there at the perfect time. I really had no words to say after, but I know that the Lord was there for us. Even though we are secretaries and dont get to work in the field that much, I am thankful for the chances we still have to see the Lord´s hand in our lives.
    
Thanks for everything,
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

Thanks for sharing the experience of how the Lord's tender mercies helped Elder Suarez in a time of need. Thank you very much for your long hours and hard work in managing the finances of the Mission. I don't know what I do without you.
--Pte. Hansen
