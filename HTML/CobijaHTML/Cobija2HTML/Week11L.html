Week 11 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 04 Ago 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This week was quite a bit more productive. In the office I got a good amount of work done. By talking with Elder Christensen we figured out something that is going to help the mission budget a lot more. The best part of the week was actually working in the field. Saturday we had a follow up lesson with a less active. He has been less active for a year or so, but before that he was a ward secretary and even was a member of the bishopric. He knows everything, but has personal issues within the church that made him not want to go. However, after having him promise to come to church, we waited on Sunday for him to church. When he walked in, I just felt so happy. He was smiling while he walked in, and you could tell he was happy to be back. If we can keep working with him, he should be a great help for the ward as well as in the work for us. He knows the whole ward, and loves teaching, so it sounds like he wants to help us in the work. On Sunday we worked pretty hard, walking all around and contacting a bunch of people. The reference that you received from Elder Vladimir Campero lives in our area, so we passed by and were able to give him a BoM and set up another appointment for later this week. Even though I was super tired at the end of Sunday, I realized that working just gives me a good feeling. Even if it doesnt turn out perfectly, I know that I have done my part. I am excited for this upcoming week.
    
Thank you for everything.
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

Thanks for letting me know about the referral from Elder Campero.
--Pte. Hansen
