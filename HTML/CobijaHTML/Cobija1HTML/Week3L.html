Week 3 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 09 Jun 2014

Dear President Dyer
    This was a very stressful week, with the audit and all that. I am really glad to be coming into the offices after Elder Gallegos, as he has set up the financial secretary´s job so well that it is an easy transition for me. I feel like I am starting to get the hang of it, but it will be somewhat daunting once Elder Gallegos leaves. We have had some troubles getting out and working as much as we should, but I did have one cool experience that I hope turns out well. The other day we got into a taxi and found a wallet. I opened it and, to my surprise, found $100 US dollars and an ID card. Well, we (Elder Gallegos and I), spent a good chunk of time on Saturday looking up the owner on the internet. It wasnt actually too hard to find her, as she is a professional artist with her own webpage. I called her, explained the situation, and said that I would send her an email (we forwarded it to you) to get in contact in order to the ID back. We took advantage of the opportunity and sent a small message about the church and shared with her a video. Who knows if anything will happen, but I trust that this may be an opportunity that God gave us. Even if nothing happens right now, maybe we are planting a seed. God works in very mysterious ways. Other than that, it was just a normal week. I feel like with this next week I will be fully prepared, or at least as much as I can be, to take over as secretary. Thank you for everything.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

Le quiero mucho Elder Bagley. I have kept you story in my file. Gracias por ser parte de este gran Obra de Salvación. Que Él Señor le bendiga.

Presidente Dyer
