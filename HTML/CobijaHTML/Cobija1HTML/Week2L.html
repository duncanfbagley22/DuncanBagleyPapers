Week 2 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 04 Jun 2014

Dear President Dyer
    This was a fairly uneventful week for the most part. I am just trying to get used to being in the offices. I am gradually picking up all of the responsabilities of my job and I think I understand mostly everything. I am really grateful I will have another 2 weeks with Elder Gallegos to make sure I get everything figured out. I really enjoyed our interview on Saturday. There was a lot of good advice that helped me out a lot. Even though I might not be out teaching investigators all day, I still have the opportunity to teach people here in the office. After all, I am still a missionary, and teaching is what we do. After a few initial problems with Elder Gallegos, things are running smoothly, and Elder Prado is very easy to get along with. I am excited to start to pick up more of the responsabilities here in the office and be the best secretary I can be. I really know this opportunity will be for my benefit later in life. Thank you for all that you do and for the interview on Saturday. Let me know if there is anything I can do for you.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

Favor de tener la fe que esta semana habrá muchas personas nuevas en su senda, si las busque. Le doy una promesa que así será, si las busque con diligencia y fe. ¡Pruebelo! Y vendtrán a la iglesia si las invitemos con humildad y persistencia. Gracias por su carta. Que Él Señor les bendiga.

Presidente Dyer
