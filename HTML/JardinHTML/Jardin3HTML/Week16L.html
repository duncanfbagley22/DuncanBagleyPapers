Week 16 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 12 May 2014

Dear President Dyer
    This was a pretty good week. We have our one investigator, Benito, who is progressing well and will be baptized this Saturday. He is so excited for his baptism, and he already has a good friendship with people in the ward, so he is going to be great. He enjoys every lesson and will be a great convert. We had an intercambio this week and I was able to go work in Colcapirhua with Elder Galan, and Elder Cruz went to El Paso to work with Elder Muñoz. Elder Galan is a great missionary. He never wants to waste any time and he knows what he is doing. He has a great attitude about the work. Elder Cruz was able to help Elder Muñoz as well. The last few days Elder Muñoz has been trying to use the things he learned from Elder Cruz and we are improving more and more. We had 4 investigators at church, 3 less actives, and our 2 converts. The work in El Paso is so much different than it was a few months ago. We have had problems finding new investigators, but we still are putting more emphasis on it. There is a lot of potential in this area, and we are only cracking the surface. I am enjoying the mission, and when I called home yesterday it was so different than the last time. I felt less focused on home and more focused on the mission. We are excited for this next week, especially the baptism. Thanks for everything
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

elder Bagley, gracisa por su fe y por esforzarse ser obediente. Le felicito su bautismo. I think you are doing a good job. Gracias por disfrutarse de la misión y gracias por escribirme. Que Él Señor le bendiga.

Presidente Dyer
