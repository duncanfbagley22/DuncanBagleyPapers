Week 14 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 28 Abr 2014

Dear President Dyer
    This was a very good week. There were 2 things that happened that made the week awesome. First were the baptisms we had on Saturday. We had been preparing to make the baptismal service something special for the last week and it turned out great. We had a lot of people there, and even got 7 investigators to come as well. We had 2 baptisms, Rosella (21) and Rodrigo (14). They were a result of working with a less active and the spirit basically did everything. The spirit has changed them and it shows. They will be great members. Also, Sunday night, we had a fireside for our newly called ward missionaries. A program has been set up in our ward using these ward missionaries. They are about 16 or so (from young women, young men, to even 2 pairs of senior missionaries). We talked about their responsabilities, had dinner, and all that. They have weekly goals, and the ward even made name tags that say Misionero de Barrio Jardin. We are hoping to see big results using this new program. These 2 baptisms this week were a result of going out with one of the missionaries. It was a great week, and the ward is starting to progress very well in the work of salvation. Thanks for all that you do.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

Thanks for your work in Jardin....I would call this a complete turnaround for the ward. Gracias por todo,

Presidente Dyer
