February 25, 2014

First day with a new comp. In the morning we dropped Lopez off at the airport, then went to the Universidad Stake for cambios. I met my new comp, Elder Muñoz. He's a little Ecuadoriano guy, and he's only been a member for 1 1/2 years. He's awesome though. He's a little quiet, but still awesome. We visited the circus people. Still going great, hopefully on track to be baptized. Later we visited Hna Quispe cause she called asking us to. Well, we talked for a while, and found out she's in rough shape. Her husband is drinking again and is scared he wants to abuse her daughter. She's been finding her daughter's clothes in the dad's room for no reason and is scared. We called the bishop and Gaby Siles and tried to solve everything. As missionaries we handle a lot. We visited Carlos Alarcon and heard his conversion story. Finally, we taught Braulio and Cristian. Overall, a very stressful day with the stuff with the Flia Quispe, but we got through it, so everything is good enough for now. I'm happy to have Elder Muñoz. Found out he got switched because his trainer was a macanero, so half way through the training, they transfered him to me. I'm tired now, so I'm going to sleep.

February 26, 2014

Pucha, voy a escribir today esta parte en español, solo para mostrar que puedo. Me gustó hoy dia, actualmente, fue difícil fisicalmente, pero al final me sentí muy bien por el trabajo que hicimos. Hoy, estudiamos, almorzamos, y luego empezamos a trabajar. Un joven del barrio, Alejandro, vino con nosotros hoy, todo el dia. Nos ayudó bastante. El siempre iba con los misioneros antes, entonces conoce muchas partes y personas que nosotros no conociamos. Encontramos un joven se llama Alejandro que se bautizó hace algunos meses. Él estaba menos activo, y tenía miedo de volver a la iglesia por vergüenza. Resolvimos todo y ya va a venir. Tambien encontramos la familia Nuñez. Es una hermana que se bautizó hace 7 años más o menos, pero despues se fue a Chaparre, por 7 años. Justamente, vovlio viernes, la encontramos, y tiene 4 nietos que viven alla que no son miembros. Visitamos a Jacqueline luego, y despues se acabó la noche. Caminamos muchísimo hoy, pero es mejor cuando Alejandro, Muñoz, y yo hablábamos. Con Hernandez, eso nunca pasó. Mi campañero es buenismo. Su testimonio es super fuerte y siempre trae el Espiritu. Me gusta trabajar con el y sé que vamos a tener éxito. Esto es todo de hoy. Averigúe que mi distrito va a ser Jardin II y todo Colcapirhua, y eso es lo que yo quería. Si no entiendes mi español, va a disculpar, per soy Boliviano ahora.

February 27, 2014

I really like my comp. Every day gets better with him. We started out today by doing service with the Flia Murillo. We swept a bunch, put stuff in buckets, then lowered them down by pulley. It wasn't too bad, and we didn't have to work in the sun at all. We went to lunch, and almost got killed by some dogs. We left, but then went back home to give Gubler a blessing. Then we visited the circus. They are still progressing well, and should be able to be baptized. They move like every month, so we're gonna ask the ZL's if we can continue teaching when they move. I also had to call people in the district to organize a service project. I feel weird having the responsibility. We taught a few other people, then we had a lesson with Braulio and Cristian. It was great, cause Braulio got his answer and wants to be baptized. We need permission from their older brother, but then we'll be good. Really, I'm enjoying like. Muñoz is awesome, Apaza too. There's gonna be lots of success, I can feel it. I'm kinda scared for my class tomorrow, but hopefully it goes well.
