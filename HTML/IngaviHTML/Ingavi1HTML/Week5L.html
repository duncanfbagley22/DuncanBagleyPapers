Week 5 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 15 Sep 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This week had some positives and negatives. The positive was incredible though. I really cant express how great the conference with Elder Waddell was. There were so many things that he said that were perfect for me. Right at the beginning he talked about how we are called to SERVE the Lord, it just differs depending on where we are called. I remember when Pres Dyer had asked me to be the financial secretary, I didnt really want to. I was just getting the hang of missionary work and I wanted to keep progressing in that. Obviously, that´s not what the Lord needed me to do. I need to be in the office because I cant receive the experiences anywhere else. I also liked the emphasis that he put on baptizing converts, not just numbers. It is really easy to slip into the mindset where we just want baptisms to tell people we have baptisms. The ultimate goal is to help the church grow and really convert people. I could go on and on about the great things that Elder Waddell said. I am 100% certain he is a man called of God. The rest of this week was fairly slow. We went out and worked but nothing was really going for us. We are still looking for the most effective way to work in the field and work in the office at the same time. The hard part is finding people, but I know we arent doing as well as we should. Thank you for all that you do and for your words in the zone conference.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

I’m glad you learned a lot from the conference with Elder Waddell. I did too. You are a very important leader in this mission.
