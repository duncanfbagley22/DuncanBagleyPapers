Week 6 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 22 Sep 2014

President Hansen,
    As I assume it was for you, this was a very busy week for us in the office. We didnt get to work a single minute in the field, due to all of the office work brought by the transfers and the end of the month. At times the office is hard. It actually is more tiring than the field and isnt quite as gratifying. I have learned a lot of things here though. When Elder Waddell mentioned that our transfers are divinely inspired and that we need to be where we are, it really felt like he was talking to me. I couldve been somewhere else, but I needed to be here. I´m enjoying life with Elder Christensen, we don´t ever have any major problems or anything, and he helps me a lot. I really dont have much to say about this week. The one good thing is that on Friday we had so many crazy things come at us, but we just trusted that it would all get resolved, which it did. I am really excited for this week. I enjoy meeting all of the new missionaries and seeing some go home. Sorry for not having much to say, but I know this week will be better. Thank you for everything that you and Hermana Hansen do for us, and let me know if there is anything we can do.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

Thanks for your letter. And thanks for being such a reliable, trustworthy, dedicated financial secretary and an outstanding missionary who is universally loved and respected by the other missionaries (and by your mission president and his sweet companion).
