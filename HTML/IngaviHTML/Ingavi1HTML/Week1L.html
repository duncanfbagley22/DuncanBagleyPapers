Week 1 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 18 Ago 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This was a very busy week. With all the cambios, missionaries leaving, and missionaries coming, we were pretty occupied. We were pretty short on sleep too. I always enjoy picking up the new missionaries. They are always so excited and bring a good energy to the mission. I feel like there are a lot of missionaries in this group. We didn´t really make our cambio between the secretaries until Wednesday, after all the business of the cambios cleared up. I definitely am excited to be with Elder Christensen, it´s just that it takes a little while for me to get accustomed to change. It is so different to finally be with someone that I dont feel like I need to tell him what to do. He just gets it, so I dont really see us having problems. The ward is good, but the excitement for missionary work is pretty low. It is weird too, because the Hermanas from our ward have had a lot of success recently, but the members are still a little dry. We are trying to brainstorm to think of what we can do. We did have one awesome experience this week. Saturday we went around looking to contact references and old investigators, but we couldnt find anything for some good time. Then, while knocking a door of an old investigator, we found a great family. They are from India and dont speak spanish, just english. Perfect for 2 Northamericans. We think they are presbyterian missionaries, but they are very open. They are some of the nicest people I have ever met. We have an appointment with them tonight (Monday) so hopefully things go well. We left and we just felt so good about them. We dont want to assume itll be perfect, but we have faith that this could turn into something good.
    
Thanks for everything,
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

Thanks for your letter. Glad to hear you are enjoying working with Elder Christensen.
--Pte. Hansen
