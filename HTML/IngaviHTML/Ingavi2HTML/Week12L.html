Week 12 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 03 Nov 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This was a tough week in the field, but it was a good one at the same time. With the cambios we were pretty busy. We planned to work all day yesterday, but when we found out about having to send people on flota and all that it made things difficult. We didnt have a ton of time to work this week, but we did have success. We have one investigator named Andres, I think I mentioned him last week. He is incredible. During the first 2 lessons, I wasnt able to go because of office duties. Everytime that Elder Christensen and Elder Davis got back from a lesson, they would just go on and on about how great he is. Yesterday I had a chance to teach him after church and he is just amazing. He understands everything and makes connections to understand. He loved the plan of salvation and one point just exclaimed "Wow, este plan es perfecto". He has come to church twice, he goes to institute, and is golden. You met him yesterday (he was a kid in a bowtie that was with us). He explained how he almost didnt go to church on Sunday, but he realized that the rain was just a challenge of faith and that we need to obey God´s commandments no matter what. He´s just amazing. As far as the cambios, I am excited. Elder Vega is going to be great. Elder Hansen is a good guy, and I think he´ll get the hang of things quick. Having Elder Christensen in Sucre will help too. The Zone Leaders there work with the airport, and Elder Christensen knows more about the airport than anyone in the whole mission because of his time in the office. He´ll be able to help us even when he´s not here. It´s probably an ideal spot for him. Anyways, thank you for everything, let me know if you need anything.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

I'm excited about Andres. Keep me posted about his progress. I love to see the secretarios having success in the field and getting out of the office as much as possible. I know you will train Elder Vega well. He carries a very good spirit with him, and I know he is a brain. It will be hard to let you go, but at the same time I am excited to get you back in the field full time.
