Week 8 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 05 Oct 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This was a decent week I guess. The highlight of the week was general conference. I did a lot of thinking this week. I felt like I just hit a rut in the work, I dont really have anything great going on. I was hoping that conference could help me out and it really did. The talk that ultimately helped me out was Elder Uchtdorf´s talk in Priesthood session where he spoke about "Lord, is it I?". I think a lot of times we just look at the defects of others, not our own. Just listening to that, combined with other talks, such as Elder Andersen´s, I realized that I need to improve in a lot of ways. Instead of worrying about things around me that I can´t change, I need to worry about things that I can change. I´ve grown a lot on my mission, but I still have a long ways to go. I also had a great spiritual feeling this morning, just studying about Christ. The scripture that talks about "feasting upon the words of Christ" is so true. Just reading his words calms me down and helps solve any problem. I love being a missionary, it´s just helping me in so many ways. There´s nowhere I would rather be. Thank you for everything, and please let me know if there is anything you think I can improve on or anything that I can do for you.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

You are one of the most important and trusted missionaries in this entire mission. I appreciate so very much your dedication, patience, and thoroughness in doing this work.
