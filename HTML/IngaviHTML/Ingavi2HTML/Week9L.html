Week 9 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 13 Oct 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This wasnt the most eventful week. We got out of the office a lot actually, but nothing really went our way in the field. Seeing as Sunday is a day where we get to work in the field, this week didnt help a lot. We´ve been going around asking members if they know any less actives or recognize names from the list of members, but it hasnt really produced any fruits. No one seems to know anybody here. We did have a cool experience, where we picked a member to visit, and when we got there she said "What a miracle, I was wondering when you were going to come" I guess she really wanted a visit, and was talking earlier that day about how no one visits her. We were able to give a blessing to her mother, and it was good to be able to help someone out. On Sunday, I had a lot of time to think. I realized the importance of our examples on everyone around us. We can really help people out just through how we act and what we say. We can be someone´s hero without even knowing it. I decided to try to pay attention to everyone around me and do what I can to help. Thank you for everything, and let me know if you need anything from me.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

What a nice experience of being guided to visit someone whose heart was yearning for a visit.
