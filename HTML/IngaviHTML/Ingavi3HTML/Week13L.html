Week 13 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 10 Nov 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This was a very busy week. With all of the cambios and consejo de lideres, we probably had one day where we got to "sleep in" until 6:30. It was a little bit stressful (I think that had a big influence on Elder Davis being sick), but we got through it. Elder Vega has been great so far. He is a really smart kid and will do very well in the office. Even though I didnt have much time to "train" him, just we had to get things done really quickly, he´s been able to pick up things very quickly. He´s also reliable, so we wont have any problems there. Elder Hansen is doing well, I think he´s just ready to get out on his own, Elder Calderon is making things a little bit more difficult sometimes. Andres is still doing great. We were able to have 2 lessons with him this week and he came with us to the 50yr anniversary celebration on Saturday. He´s already talking about a mission and getting the priesthood. He´s great. We talked to him this morning and he said he wanted to see us today. He said something about a weird dream that scared him or something. We´ll see what happens, hopefully everything is ok. Anyways, I know I´m going to enjoy this cambio with Elder Davis and Elder Vega. Thanks for everything and let me know if there is anything you need.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

PLease let me know in advance when you have a firm baptism date with Andres as I would love to attend if I can.
