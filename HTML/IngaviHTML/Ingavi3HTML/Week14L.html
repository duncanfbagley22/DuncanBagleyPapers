Week 14 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 17 Nov 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This was a pretty solid week. I´m enjoying being able to train elder Vega. He understands everything well and really just wants to learn as quickly and as well as he can. He´s got a good attitude and enjoys working in the field as well. We had a pretty good week in the field as well. Andres is still doing great. We taught him about tithing and fasting this because he had a lot of questions. After explaining fasting we landed on the idea of fasting with him, because he has his finals this week and wants to do well. We fasted from Saturday to Sunday and it was a great experience. He really embraced the idea and loved it. We have a tentative date for Nov 29th, but we´ll confirm it this week and let you know. He´s really excited about baptism. A cool thing happened this week on Sunday. About 3 months ago we made contact with the family of a less active member that seemed interested in the gospel (I believe I´ve mentioned it in a letter). Anyways, we haven´t been able to find them since. Everytime we either go and no one is there or they fall through on appts. We´ve kept trying though and yesterday we finally found them. Her name is Erica and her daughter Angela and there are great. They want to find a church, they´ve been to an LDS church before and already know a lot, and they have family that are members. I was just so happy that after perservering so long we finally found them and they are great. Anyways, thanks for everything, and let me know if there is anything that I can do for you.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

Thanks, Elder Bagley. Keep up the great work.
