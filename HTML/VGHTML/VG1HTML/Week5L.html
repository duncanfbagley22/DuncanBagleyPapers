Week 5 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 19 Ene 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This was a great week. On tuesday, Elder Laidlaw and I were talking about what we could do with the zone and how to help them that day. We decided to set a goal for the zone, and the first thing that came to mind was lessons with a member. We decided that we would shoot for 100. In the weeks prior, we were averaging around 50. It kind of seemed like a long shot, but we had faith that it could work. We worked all week long on our share of the goal. We figured that the zone needed to average 8 lessons with a member per companionship. We set our companionship goal as 10, and we focused on that all week. We really saw quite a few blessings come from that goal. Especially on Sunday. We had 7 at the start of the day, and we needed to get 3 more. Through just looking for people to teach, as all of our plans and back up plans fell through, we ended up finding 2 new people that were really prepared to hear the gospel. We found a bunch of good people this week and we really saw the progress building in our area. As far as the zone, they went above and beyond the goal. We ended up reaching 114 lessons with a member this week, an average of 8.7 per companionship. The part that made me the happiest is that there were companionships that usually average 1 or 2 lessons with a member per week, and they ended up reaching 8 or 9 this week. The zone worked really really hard and we are super proud of each of them. Seeing now what the zone is capable of, we are hoping to see the success continuing to grow. We have great missionaries, and the zone is finally starting to become unified. It really was probably the best week for us and a companionship and as a zone that I've had in my time here. Thank you for everything and let us know if there is anything we can do for you.

President Hansen Response

What a tremendous accomplishment by your zone. Thanks for your inspired leadership. I'm really proud of you and Elder Laidlaw for your leadership and your entire zone for their hard work and determination to achieve a worthy and challenging goal.
