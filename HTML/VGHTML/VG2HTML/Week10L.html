Week 10 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 23 Feb 2014

Dear President Hansen,
    This was quite the week. I personally didnt really work much in our area. Just on Sunday I worked a whole day here in Villa Graciela. The other days were spent staying inside for carnaval, zone conference, a day full of meetings, and finally 2 intercambios. I cant speak much on how the area is going, I'm sure Elder Hansen will give you more info. It does seem like it's still improving though. I can speak on my personal improvement and how the intercambios went. First I went with Elder Morales in Frutillar. One thing I'm learning about every intercambio is how there is so much more to some people than we initially know. Elder Morales has had a rough family life recently, but he is a great missionary. He has a great testimony and is obedient. He's had a string of "trunky" companions recently, but he really works hard. That area has been rough on them, but they do have some good people to teach. We also found a couple that hopefully do well. I'm really glad he's in my zone. I also went on an intercambio with Elder Mickle in Villa Moscu. I learned soooo much. It was cool because I lived with him my first transfer in the mission, so we already got along, but we both have grown up a lot. One thing I learned was to contact better. Elder Mickle asks a lot of good questions and contacts everyone. He is a huge example to me and a great assistant. He is really humble too. It was one of the better intercambios I've ever done. Elder Hansen learned a lot from Elder Vasquez as well, and we both tried to put it to use on Sunday when we were back together. We contacted more and found more people. If we open our mouths, God really does put people in our path. We had another one of those moments yesterday where we knocked "one more door" and we found a really good new guy. I learned a lot in the conference and Elder Hansen and I are trying to consecrate ourselves. I'm excited to have a full week to work now. Thanks for everything, and let me know if there's anything we can improve on.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

Thanks for the feedback on the awesome Elder Mickle. That's really good to know that he is doing excellent intercambios. I also really liked your feedback on Elder Morales. Do you see him as a future zone leader?
