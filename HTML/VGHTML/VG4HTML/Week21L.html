Week 21 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 11 May 2014

Dear President,
    I dont know why, but the Lord has decided to bless us sooo much this transfer. This was honestly one of the best weeks of my whole mission. I know it is the Lord's hand doing everything, because we are working just as hard as I did with Elder Hansen, but the success is just pouring down. We had so many miracles, so I dont know which one to share. The best part of the week was yesterday. We were sitting at church and just sat there a bit amazed at how many investigators showed up. We had 9 people there. We are seeing so much success here. From more inspired dreams to knocking doors and finding inspired people, to seeing a person I helped reactivate give a talk in church, everything is working. I now know that "si sembramos lo bueno, cosecharemos lo bueno". I'm just so happy about it all. One experience I liked was non saturday. We knocked a door (in a part of the area where we've never been) and met a pastor of another church. He was nice, but he kept telling us that we needed to rethink what we were doing, that religions are wrong. He said there was no way to test if the BoM is true. Well, about five minutes after we talked to him, we came upon another house. We knocked it and found one guy that was baptized in Spain and his cousin who isnt baptized. They had the biggest smiles ever when they saw us. As we walked out of the lesson I just turned to Elder Bradford and said "there's our proof that this church is true". Honestly, I couldnt be happier with the area right now. The zone is still struggling, especially Arocahua (but you know all about that). Things are going well and I know that I will end my mission on a high note. Let us know if there is anything you need us to do or help you with.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

I could not be happier for you and Elder Bradford. The Lord is rewarding you both for your patience and diligence, especially you who have been there much longer. So nice to see such a great elder as you being blessing with golden investigators and finishing your mission with such a strong area.
