Week 22 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 18 May 2014

Dear President,
    So, the miracles continue. This week we saw it in some less actives that we finally got reactivated. It is the Flia Garcia. The dad is the one that was supposed to speak in the charla fogonera in the stake, but when he didnt they started going downhill. But, they have been progressing recently and this week they finally had their temple recommend interviews with the stake president and bishop. They are now able to go back and do endowments and everything. We are going to help them with family history work and then on Friday we'll go with them to the temple for the first time in a few years. They could be a powerhouse family for the ward, so we just need to get them callings and stuff now. They have such great testimonies. It's awesome to see all the work paying off. If there's anything I've learned this transfer, is that we truly do reap what we sow. Just every day new things happen that make me stop and just smile at how blessed we are. We dont even focus on getting great numbers, they just come on their own. The Lord really does love me, I'm sure of that. We also did an intercambio with Huayllani this week. I was able to go with Elder Muñoz. We were companions for 2 transfers about a year ago, so it was fun to relive everything. Those 3 months were some of the best of my misión, just as we saw so many miracles. You know as well as I do (well, probably better) the problems he has had, but he really is a great kid. He just needs companions to watch out for him. He was one of my favorite companions (honestly, I have had so many great companions that most of them are my favorites), and he has a really strong testimony. Anyways, I'm excited to get going on this week and see what we can do. I know that the Lord will keep blessing us. Thanks for everything
    
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

I couldn't be happier for you, Elder Bagley. You have worked so hard in your time as a ZL. It's really great to see you now reaping blessings. Congratulations on the quality rescue, and for all you have done to build up your own area and your entire zone.
I really like Elder Munoz as well. He has a sincerity about him that I find very powerful.
