Week 20 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 04 May 2014

Dear President,
    This was a great week. First off we learned a lot from the consejo de lideres. I always enjoy those. We are also really happy with the zone this week. The zone as a whole improved a ton. We are especially happy with the progress that Elder Morales in making, as well as Elder Reque. Ever since we sat them down and talked, they've been doing a lot better. All of the numbers went up. I dont really know what the difference is, but the zone just worked a lot harder this week. The only real problems are with Arocahua, which you already know all about. We finished the numbers last night and we were just really happy with the zone. As far as the area, it is going great. We have 2 investigators, the mother daughter combo, who are doing very well. We met them because with Elder Hansen we were very persistent. We just knocked the door and when they told us they were busy washing their dog, we just insisted on helping them. They didnt believe that we would do it, but we went in and were about to start when they told us to stop and that they would listen. We've been using the members a lot with them; we've had lessons in their home with 7 different members. The daughter was really excited to tell us about how she prayed about the BoM and had a dream. In the dream, she said that she was following Elder Bradford. She didnt know where she was going, but she said that there was a bright white light wherever she was being lead. Elder Bradford quickly brought out 1 Nephi 8 and we talked about Lehi's dream and the tree of life. We're hoping that both of them can be baptized in a few weeks. Anyways, thanks for everything and let us know if you need anything from us.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Hansen Response

I think Bolivians are more in tune with inspired dreams than the average person. It's very interesting how often the Lord communicates with them through dreams.
