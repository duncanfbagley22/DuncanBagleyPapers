Week 20 (December 16, 2013)

Solo voy a escribir el título en español para mostrar que puedo hablar, también no tengo una idea creativa por mi título

Maimantajamunki Familia,
That is quechua for where are you from. I just wanted to show off my quechua skills. This week was pretty good. Our numbers didnt fall too much, even though we had no time to work on friday, and other annoying things we had to do during the week. We are starting to grow some success. Me and Elder Pinta and doing great. His english isnt awful, and he talks slow for a Colombian. The interesting things that happened this week... First I guess I will talk about our good 3 investigators. First off is Dennys. He went to youth conference this week, so hopefully that helped. We have an appointment this tuesday, so the date will be set then. He is also gonna help with the ward christmas party. I feel confident, but you never know what can happen. Second we have Dina. She is super cool. She is an old catholic lady, but one of the few nice ones. Really, catholic ladies arent very nice here. She has been to church twice, and we even pulled a little string in the ward that will help, she is now gonna start working for the relief society president in the house, so we have literally done everything. She doesnt have a date... yet, but she will on wednesday. Finally, we have Karla. She is the ´´friend´´ of our YMs president. She technically lives in a different ward, but since she knows everyone here now and the elders over there arent too great, she is ours. I put a date last wednesday, but it scared her. But but but, she read 2 Ne 31 and prayed and now she feels good about baptism. She just needs her dad to be ok with it. It is literally impossible to have an easy baptism. Send as many prayers this way to get her dad to soften his heart to accept the baptism. Other than that we had interviews and a zone conference this week. President Dyer is just so cool. He makes me feel good and realize what I need to do at the same time. He also spoke in conference, along with Hna Dyer the assistants and some other missionaries. It was really good, and they also gave us christmas presents. Oh yeah, the package came and some letters from Edie and Bishop. At the conference there was a push up contest, and president was included. He is kinda old, but still can crank out push ups. Other than that, just regular stuff this week. We played a lot of futsal today. I put on some shoes I found in the house. Bad idea, they are size 10 1/2, a bit too small. Life is going good. I feel good about the work I have done here. I´m not perfect, but I am growing every day. I am about a 4th of the way done, and that is crazy. I am gonna blink and it is gonna end. Overall, life is good. Really sad about Craig, but I have no doubt he is in a better place. Plan of Salvation is real. Love you all, and it is exciting that in 9 DAYS we get to talk. Still havent heard anything. Pretty sure it will be skype, so dont worry. We will have all the info by next week. El evangelio es super verdadero.

Les amo,
Elder Bagley
