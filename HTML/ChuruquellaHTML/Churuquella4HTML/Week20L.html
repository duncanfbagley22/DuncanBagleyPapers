Week 20 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 16 Dic 2013

Dear President Dyer,
    This was a pretty good week. We had a conference and interviews and things going on in the ward that made it so we couldnt teach all the time, but the numbers didnt drop off. They are about the same, and for the time we were given to work this week, we are feeling pretty good. Even though we had a lot of lessons fall through, it was still a good week. We have 3 investigators that are progressing really well. I was told you called out Dennys Medina during the youth conference, so that is funny. We just need to have a firm date with him, and we will be good. He is ready for baptism, and I bet the youth conference really helped. We also have a lady named Dina. She is catholic, like everyone, but she is really interested. We are doing everything we can to get the members involved, even as far as having Dina work in the house of our Relief Society President. She is great and keeps all her commitments. Finally, we have an investigator named Karla. We popped the baptism question last time and it kind of scared her. After reading 2 Ne 31 and praying, she wants to be baptized now. Just shows me that the Spirit is the important thing to convince the investigators. We cant do anything without it. The conference was great, and I learned a lot. The interview too. I love getting personalized help. Something Elder Wright or Knight, dont remember, said was interesting and made me think. The fact that we should leave our areas, investigators, wards, and companions better than when we got here. I might not have as many baptisms as the next guy, but I feel like I have had a positive influence. I feel like Elder Pinta will be left with a lot to work with when I leave. It is hard to feel positive at times, but I have been doing what Hna Dyer challenged us to do, writing down what we are thankful for, and that helps I think. Overall, I feel like I have progress so much since I got here, and I hope I can always keep progressing and getting better, as that is what life is about. Thanks for everything.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

Thanks for your positive influence. Gracias por me haber escrito. Que Él Señor le bendiga

Presidente Dyer
