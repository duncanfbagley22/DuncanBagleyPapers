Week 19 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 09 Dic 2013

Dear President Dyer,
    This was a really good week. I am really starting to see some things pay off finally. We found a lot of success this week, at least compared to the other weeks. Partly I think it is because we have been trying to be more obedient, and I think the Lord is blessing us. We just recently had our investigator tell us he thinks he is ready for baptism. Before his family was opposed, but for some reason now they are in favor of it. We also found an awesome investigator named Dina. She is an older woman that lives alone. She is going through a lot of problems in her life. The owners of her house want to throw her out, and she is looking for a place to live. She has also recently lost a 5 year old son, an 18 year old son, and her mother. We found her knocking doors, and she is progressing fast. She has come to church and even came to a Relief Society activity. We brought a member with us and that helped a lot. The members really are the key. We also received a reference from the YMs president in our ward and she is great. She has come to church a bunch and is really curious. Overall, I just feel really blessed this week. Everyone we have talked to have been great for the most part. We have a lot of future investigators that we have contacted, just waiting for them to return from vacations. Me and Elder Pinta are getting along great as well. We dont have any problems and he already knows so much. I am excited for the conference this week. Thanks for everything.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

Gracias por la carta Elder Bagley. You have been together a long time. I commend you for your ability to get along well.

Presidente Dyer
