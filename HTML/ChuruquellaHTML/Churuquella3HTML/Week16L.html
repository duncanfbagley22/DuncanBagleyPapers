Week 16 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 18 Nov 2013

President Dyer,
    Well, this week started off pretty well with the news that I was getting a companion. Then I found out that I have to train him, and that makes me a little bit nervous. I just finished up my training, and at times I don´t trust myself to know enough to help train him. The good thing is that he is a good missionary already. He is still getting used to the life in Bolivia, but it is coming. Overall, I don´t feel like I am training him at times, but that it is the other way around. We have already had some good moments this week. We taught a few good lessons with members present, and found some new investigators. We still have a lot of work to make up from the week I lost when I had to go to Cochabamba, but we have been trying to work with the members a lot more. We got blessed this week when 1) our ward mission leader returned from his 2 month trip so we finally have someone more to help us 2) A new family moved into the ward and they brought 3 young men, something the ward desperately needs. Hopefully these, and a few other improvements that we are trying to make will hopefully bring some results. Of our best investigators, nearly all of them our references, so that is what we are going to focus on more. Overall, this week brought a big change, but hopefully with the help of the Lord I can do it. Thanks for everything you do, and I will hopefully have a letter full of success next week.
Until then,     
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

Los nuevos que se encuentren esta semana, hasta el fin de noviembre, tendrán el mejor potencial de bautizarse este año. Favor de enforcarse en encontrar nuevos.

Haga una pureba. En el DyC84:88 dice, "Y quienes os reciban, allí estaré yo también, porque iré delante de vuestra faz. Estaré a vuestra diestra y a vuestra siniestra, y mi Espíritu estará en vuestro corazón, y mis ángeles alrededor de vosotros, para sosteneros." Yo lo creo,...entonces, vaya adelante y reclame su premio. Y sepa que las bendiciónes vendrán despues de la prueba de la fe.

Le quiero mucho. Estoy orando para su exito.

Presidente Dyer
