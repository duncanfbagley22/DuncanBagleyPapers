Week 13 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 28 Oct 2013

Dear President Dyer,
    This week was really interesting. First of all, getting a new companion is really different. Supayabe is great, but obviously he is different from Elder Lopez. He is a great elder though. I think with him we could get some work done. Only problem is that obviously he is in Cochabamba right now. It is a really difficult situation. His hernia had us moving slowly, so we didnt get much done this week. It really was hard on me this week. I have been having a hard time with the lack of success we have been having here, and now it is worse. With Supayabe gone, it will be hard to get some work done. I am going to try to go out with members, but we will see how that goes. I have learned a lot from the situation though. I hope the Elder Supayabe can get back healthy soon, cause he is a good missionary. I know that these things happen for a reason, I just want to know what the reason is. However, even with a companion in the hospital, and with just me in my area, I will keep going. I know that everything is part of the Lord´s plan, I just need to be patient. Overall, the week was interesting. Sending 2 companions to Cochabamba and all, but the mission is still great. I am excited to try and be creative in order to have a good week. Thanks for everything.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

Gracias por no perder el tiempo del Señor. Su meta es bautizar dos personas por mes. ¿Aceptará la meta? Favor de contestarme esta pregunta en su proxima carta. Que Él Señor le bendiga.

Presidente Dyer
