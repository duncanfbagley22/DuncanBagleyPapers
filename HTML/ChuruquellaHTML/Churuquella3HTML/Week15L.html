Week 15 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 11 Nov 2013

Dear President Dyer,
    This week was pretty difficult. Being without a companion has been pretty rough. Up until Thursday, I wasn´t able to work very much. Thursday I got the opportunity to go with Elder Herndon and Elder Cusiatao as they helped me in my area. They helped me a lot. We found some people that I am going to teach and had a lesson. I learned a lot from them and they were good examples for me. I havent had that much time to work, as I have to go with a different set of elders everyday, but I always had to go home early so I could meet up with the other elders that I live with. This experience without a companion has made me a lot more grateful for the fact that I will have a companion. It has been really hard at times, but there have been some bright spots. Friday I was with Elder Long and Elder Diazgranados. We were just waiting for Elder Espina and Elder Harris to finish up a lesson so I could go home with them, so me, Elder Long and Elder Diazgranados waited outside. We were seated on a bench and this girl came and asked if she could sit down. Her little brother was playing with a basketball, so I started to play with him. I love playing basketball, and not to brag, but I am decent enough. I started dribbling around with him and we became good friends. We then started to talk to his older sister just about what we were doing here. We werent even in my area, but we found this girl that lives in my area and we got all her information and hopefully I can go teach her this week. I just loved seeing the hand of the Lord in my life, and also being able to use my talents to help find people. The good experiences were few and far between, but they still happen. I am excited to get back to work this week, and my goal is to have more lessons this week than I have had in the first 3 combined. Anyways, thanks for all the help, especially in the stake conference this week.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

You are the trainer for your new compañion. I think he still lacks half of of his training. Please figure out where he is in his training and get the zone leaders to train you on how to be a trainer.

Yo le veo a usted como un gran misionero, obediente, diligente y exitoso.

Que tenga una buena semana y que El Espiritu le acompañe. Le queremos mucho.

Presidente y Hermana Dyer
