Week 5 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 02 Sep 2013

Hola Presidente,
    This week was probably the most successful one that I have had on my mission. In our first 4 sundays, we had only one investigator attend church. Yesterday we had 5, and one of them even got up to bare his testimony. I was feeling like the work was slow here and I wasnt gonna get anything done, but finally we are starting to get somewhere. I still have moments where I am homesick and I think too much about the time here being too long, but there are those special moments where I realize how important it is to be here. Right now we have about 6 investigators that I am feeling really good about. We have more, but those 6 are solid. We have one named Hector that is awesome. His mother is a recent convert, and we hadnt talked to him before, but now he is just moving quickly. He believes in the church and Joseph Smith, and we taught about the Book of Mormon yesterday and I can tell he wants to read it. He says he always feels different with the church related things, that he knows something is there. We also have the familia Leon. The father, mother, 2 daughters, and son. The father was challenged to read the introduction... and he ended up reading half of first Nephi. The son is amazing. He described the prayer he had to find out if the church is true, and the spirit was so strong. He is 12 and he bore his testimony in church. The family is just amazing. Overall, things are continually improving. Elder Lopez is great. He helps me out so much and at times he just has the spirit so strong in lessons and can touch peoples hearts. I am starting to gain more confidence in myself, the ability I have to follow the spirit, and confidence in my spanish. The other day I was apparently sleep talking in Spanish. I am nowhere near perfect, and I definitely have a long ways to go, but I am glad to see I am improving. I am so grateful to have Sucre as my first area and it is a little weird I have been here over a month. The work is the Lord´s work and I can see that everyday. Thanks for all you do Presidente.
    Love,
      Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

Great experiences. Gracias por su constancia. Gracia por esforzarse ser un mensajero verdadero de Jesucristo. Que El Señor le bendiga.

Presidente Dyer
