Week 9 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 30 Sep 2013

Presidente Dyer,
    This week was tough. We came into the week thinking everything was going to be easy and getting the numbers we want is easy, but that isn´t the case. Tuesday was the hardest day of the week. We worked the entire day. We walked around at one point, 6 or 7 hours, with nothing. We had a few appointments that day and every single thing fell through. We tried to be perfectly obedient that day too. We talked to countless people and knocked dozens of doors, with nothing. We probably worked our hardest that day, and we had nothing to show for it. Wednesday started off the same way too. We worked hard, followed all the rules, but nothing. Then all of the sudden, around 6, Elder Lopez just started walking, looking determined. We literally went through our entire area, like a 20min walk, until we stopped at a door. Elder Lopez had looked so determined, that I thought he knew where he was going. I asked him who lived at the house and he said he had no idea. We knocked on the door, a woman answered, and we got invited in and had a solid lesson with her. She was super prepared and said she had been praying for some sort of help or answer, and she said she thought that answer was us. It helped lift my spirits and testimony, as there is no way Elder Lopez was directing us, but it was the spirit. It was the type of miracle that we needed this week. After wednesday, things started to pick up and now we are starting to get somewhere. We are pushing hard to get success, and praying hard. I know that this week was a test for us, to see if when things were going bad we would just give up and do our own thing, or if we would continue to trust in the Lord and His guidance. I was getting a bit too prideful, thinking the mission is easy and numbers are easy. This week humbled me and made me realize that I cant do anything without the Lord. We are starting to make more process now, but it all starts with what the Lord wants. I am excited to implement the things I learned this week and I am really excited for General Conference. Thanks for everything.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

you seem to be doing great. Thanks for the letter. Gracias por venir a la conferencia especial esta semana. El mensaje más fuerte que recibí de Elder Calderón es que tenemos que mejorarnos en the manera de trabajar con los miembros. Le queremos mucho. Que Él Señor le benidga.

Presidente Dyer
