Week 8 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 23 Sep 2013

Dear Presidente Dyer,
    This week was a bit interesting. Each day had something really different. Definitely the best part of the week was that we had our first baptism. The kid we baptized, Hector, was prepared and now he and his whole family are members of the church. He talked about how he felt like a different person after the baptism and he had had his life changed by the gospel. We had a little trouble with filling the font, so we had to go to the church at 6am saturday morning to get the baptism started on time. The water heater was broken too, so we ended up filling probably 30% of the font by hand using the water heaters that we have in the apartment. But, with the help of Elder Cedano and Elder Long, we got it filled and had the baptism. Also, this week I got to go on an intercambio with Elder Cedano. I learned a lot from him and we tried to implement a lot of things that Elder Calderon told us. That conference was great by the way. It felt like everything Elder Calderon said was something that me and Elder Lopez need to implement right away. This past week we didnt have too much success with finding new investigators or getting them to come to church, but me and Elder Lopez had a great weekly planning session, and with the goals and things we have in mind, I feel like this week we are going to have a good one. No conference, no cambios, just work, and we are excited for that. My spanish is coming along great and I think in spanish a lot of the now, and there is plenty of evidence that the gift of tongues is real. Overall, life has been pretty good, ready to get back to work. I have received so many blessings I cant count them. The mission gets better every day. Hasta Lunes
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

Le invito que pida una bendición de su Obispo en el barrio donde esta ahora. El tiene la llaves para su barrio y el le podría ayudar en este barrio. Pida al Obispo 15 nombres para ayudar con los demas miembros del consejo del barrio.

Estoy animado por la entrevista con usted que tendremos pronto. Favor de enfocarse trabajar más con los miembros. Le queremos mucho. Que los angeles le cuiden.

Presidente Dyer
