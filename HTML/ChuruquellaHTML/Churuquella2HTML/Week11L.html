Week 11 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 14 Oct 2013

Presidente Dyer,
    This week was a good and a bad one. It was good because it was a fun one, but bad because I don´t feel like we got that much work done. This week we had a talent show with all the missionaries in Sucre. It was a stressful week, because there was so much to do. We were running all over the place, calling everyone, and trying to make the show the best it could be. It ended up pretty good. It was crowded and I feel like we did a really good job with everything. We had a few investigators and less actives show up too, so the goal was accomplished. The problem with the talent show was that it didnt leave a lot of time to work. Plus, I just dont feel like we were super focused this week. I feel like the more I want to work, the less we do. We did have ward counsel yesterday and I feel like we animated the people in the ward and we are going to start making progress with the people in the ward. As far as our investigators go, we are working slowly with a few. The planned baptism we had is going to fall through because we just cant seem to find a time to teach him, as every time we have it planned, it ends up falling through. We are trying to come up with a plan with the ward for missionary work. We have a couple of good ideas for finding people this week. That is the thing we are lacking is new investigators. I really want to get focused this week, as it is probably the last one with Elder Lopez and I dont wanna end this time with a low week. Hopefully we can get it going this week and get focused. Thanks for everything you do.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

Hoy en la misión tenemos tres prioridades que necesitamos para aumentar los bautismos:

Seguir aumentando el numero de los nuevos mediante trabajar con los miembros y hablar con todos,
Aumentar el porcentaje de las lecciones con miembros presente mediante trabajar con los miembros, y
Amplificar la asistencia, invitando a los miembros que participen con nosotros en traer los investigadores a la iglesia. Yo tengo confianza que usted lo hará. Le quiero mucho. Que Él Señor le bendiga.

Presidente Dyer
