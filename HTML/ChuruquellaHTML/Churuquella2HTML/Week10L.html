Week 10 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 07 Oct 2013

President Dyer,
    This week was pretty good. The best part of the week for me was conference. At home I had always watched conference, but I would only really pay attention to a few talks. This time I was paying attention the whole time, which is how it should be. I learned so much from conference. I am pretty sad that it is actually over now. I also enjoyed the interview that I had with you. It gave me some confidence, and it also helped me realize what I need to do better. As far as the work goes, this week was decent. We had 6 people come to conference, which was pretty cool. We keep struggling to have people show up to appointments, so that is something we need to improve. I had the opportunity to be senior companion 2 times, more or less, this week. We had wednesday, when all the leaders had a conference, so me Elder Long and Elder Harris went out with just us. We started in my area, so I got to lead. We actually taught a lesson, and a pretty good one at that. Also, Friday night we had 2 appointments at one time, so me and Elder Lopez split up and we each took a priesthood holder with us to a lesson. I actually taught a lesson basically by myself and tried to contact people on the street. It isnt anywhere near where I should be, but it gave me confidence with my spanish. We have talked with the Bishop about the ward mission plan and we have also received the blessings that you suggested. I think we are really going to start picking up the work with the members, and that seemed to be a theme for conference, so I think it is what we should do. We had a lot of things to do outside of work this last week, but I feel like this week we should be able to have a good work week. Thanks for everything, hasta lunes
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

you are agood missionary Elder Bagley. Gracias por ser fiel en todo. Gracias por la entrevista hace dos semanas. He leído su carta. Que Él Señor le bendiga.

Presidente Dyer
