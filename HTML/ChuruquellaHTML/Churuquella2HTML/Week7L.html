Week 7 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 16 Sep 2013

Dear President Dyer,
    This week was a bit crazy. We had a trio from tuesday to friday, first with Elder Mickle, then with Elder Cedano, and then with Elder Cusiatao. It was great to learn a little bit from each one of them, but it was a bit hectic going to the airport and the terminal a bunch of times. Saturday was good to finally just have me and elder Lopez. The work was a bit tough this week because of 2 things. 1, the cambios were crazy, as you know. We were going all over the place to pick up people and drop them off, and neither me nor Elder Lopez is even leaving. 2, this week was the entrada or Virgen of Guadalupe dance thing in Sucre. We had plenty of appointments, but a lot fell through and everyone went to the entrada. It was like the city was dead, cause there was no one. Not that these things are an excuse for less lessons and such, it was just a little crazy this week. That being said, we had some success at church. 7 investigators came to church, and they all stayed at least 2 hours. All of them are progressing too. Also, this week were are going to have our first baptism, at least in my time here. It is with a teenager named Hector, and he is beyond prepared. He even comes with us to help teach other investigators and brings his friend to church. It is great to see the fruits of the labor. Elder Lopez and I are still getting along great. We havent been studying as much as we should, and that has been tough on me at times, cause I am knew and dont know much. We have made it a goal this week to strive to study and be more punctual. This week has also been good, as I have gotten to know Elder Harris, who lives with us and is new. Talking with him helps me realize how much I have learned in just 6 weeks. I am excited to hear from Elder Calderon this week and to be uplifted. The fast really helped prepare us more, and I am excited to hear what he has to say. Overall, good week. Everything is smooth now after cambios and I am ready to push forward in the work. Thanks for everything, hasta lunes.
    
    Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

you seem to be doing great. Thanks for the letter. Gracias por venir a la conferencia especial esta semana. El mensaje más fuerte que recibí de Elder Calderón es que tenemos que mejorarnos en the manera de trabajar con los miembros. Le queremos mucho. Que Él Señor le benidga.

Presidente Dyer
