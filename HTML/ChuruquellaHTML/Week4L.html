Week 4 Mission President Letter

Weekly Letter - 26 Ago 2013

Presidente Dyer,
    Ok, so I have written the last 3 letters in spanish. That is great and stuff but I feel like I cant convey all of my feelings in spanish and let you know what is really going on. This week was the fastest one yet. Still, I have days, or mostly moments that feel really slow, but overall the week was pretty fast. My spanish is really coming along. Elder Lopez says that my spanish is at 75%. I dont know about that, but it really is a lot better. I can always understand what is going on, just not all of the words. The 3 and a half weeks here have been more useful than the CCM and the 3 years in high school. I am starting to feel a little more accustomed to Sucre too. The mornings are still hard but as the day progresses, it gets better. 2 interesting things happened this week that I would like to share. First, we found out that the husband of an investigator is being investigated by the police. We found out that he and his wife, the investigator, are now running from the police in Santa Cruz. It was kind of depressing cause she was really interested in the church and we were progressing. The other experience was with the son of a recent convert. The convert, Sandra, and her daughter Maria were baptized by Elder Boyd and Lopez about 4 or 5 weeks ago. The son, Hector, didnt attend the lessons. He is 16 and before didnt believe in God. About 2 weeks ago we had a spur of the moment lesson with him and invited him to pray about if God existed. Yesterday we went to talk to him and had a great lesson. Probably the most spiritual moment on my mission so far. He said that he knew there was something more from the peace he felt. When Elder Lopez bore his testimony on families, both Elder Lopez and Hector began to cry. The spirit was so strong. We have an appointment on wednesday, and I am sure we are on our way to baptism. That is all for this week. I am progressing every day and the moments like the ones with Hector make every moment worth it. Church is true.
    Love,
      Elder Bagley

President Dyer Response

you are doing great elder Bagley. He leído su carta. Le queremos mucho. Gracias por ser diligente y obediente.
